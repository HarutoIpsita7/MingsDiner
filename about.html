Foundation
Ming's Dinner was founded by Dr. Ghan Shayam Sundar on 19th of December 2000
Dr. Ghan Shayam Sundar's wife Mrs. Sriya Shyama Sundar's hand cooking is so tasty that everyone who even got the sent of the food got drawn towards it.
She is the main cook at Dr. Ghan Shyam Sundar's Ming's dinner. She always gives food to everyone who comes to her even if they don't have a penny. She is a motherly person under whom the restaurents has grown up. The younger cooks follow each and every instruction from lighting the fire to serving the food. After the food is made Mrs. Sriya Shyama Sundar offers the food to her Prabhu nd then to the people who come to eat. The food made at Dr. Ghan Shyam Sundar's Ming's dinner is purely Vegitarian without onion and garlic.
It is always a pleasure from the bottom of the heart both to look forward to eat dishes cooked by her, while having the food and also afterwards.
